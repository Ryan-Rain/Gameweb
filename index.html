DOWNLOAD THE GAME

A physics based 2D-platformer game developed in Unity2D where the main character Johnny Blower has been swallowed whole by a huge monster. The player assumes the role of leaf blower wielding Johnny and needs to use this single tool to blow enemies, acid, and projectiles around in order to overcome obstacles. Interact with the environment, slay enemies, and find clever ways to propel Johnny out of the stomach.

FEATURES

Here's what you can do!

Blow Acid

Blow acid on enemies or to give yourself a path to new areas.

Blow Enemies

Blow away enemies and their projectiles to avoid damage and maybe do some damage of your own.

Use Jets

Use the jets to propel yourself even higher!

Flying Enemies

Look out for sky-borne enemies attacking you from above.

Spikes

Spikes are scattered around the environment. Make sure to not get hurt!

Exit

Find your way to the next level.
